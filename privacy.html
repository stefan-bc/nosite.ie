Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 March 2026

Who we are

Nosite is a web design and hosting service operated from Dublin, Ireland. You can contact us at hello@nosite.ie.

What we collect

The only personal data we collect is what you submit through our contact form: your name, email address, phone number, and message. We do not store any data on our own servers. There are no user accounts, no cookies, and no server-side tracking.

How it's processed

Your contact form submission is handled by Formspree, a third-party form service. We use the information solely to respond to your enquiry. We also use Umami, a privacy-focused analytics tool that collects anonymous page-view statistics without cookies or personal data.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone. The only third parties involved are Formspree (form delivery) and Umami (anonymous analytics). Neither receives personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to operate.

Your rights

Under the GDPR and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, you can ask us to show, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. Email hello@nosite.ie and we will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will revise the date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
