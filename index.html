FAQ

What if the price goes up?

It won't. €29 a month is locked in for life. No surprises, no inflation adjustments. The price you start with is the price you keep - even if our model changes.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Just shoot us an email. No fee, no notice period.

Do I own my domain?

Yes. We register it in your name and keep it safe. But it's yours regardless.

Does the email address cost extra?

Yes. We can arrange a solution separately at a reasonable cost.

Can I see a demo before it's live?

Yes. That's how it works. We build a draft of your site first. We share a test version which you can access securely for review. Once you're happy, it goes live.

What if I need changes after launch?

No problem. Just email us. Most changes are done within a day.
